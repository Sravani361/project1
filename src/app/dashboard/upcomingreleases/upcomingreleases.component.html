Upcoming Releases :

1. Me Before You by Jojo Moyes

Release Date: December 25, 2024

Description: Me Before You by Jojo Moyes is a poignant romantic novel that
tells the story of Louisa Clark, a quirky young woman who takes a job as a
caregiver for Will Traynor, a wealthy man left paralyzed after an accident.
Initially struggling with his bitterness and despair, Louisa is determined to
bring joy back into Will's life. As they spend time together, an unexpected
bond forms, challenging both their perspectives on life and love. The story
explores themes of choice, disability, and the complexities of relationships,
leading to an emotional climax that questions the meaning of living fully.
It's a heartfelt tale that resonates with readers long after the last page.

2. The Silence of the Lambs by Thomas Harris

Release Date: December 20, 2024

Description: The Silence of the Lambs by Thomas Harris is a gripping psychological
thriller that follows FBI trainee Clarice Starling as she seeks the help of the
imprisoned Dr. Hannibal Lecter, a brilliant yet sinister psychiatrist and
cannibalistic serial killer. Tasked with apprehending another killer known as Buffalo
Bill, who is abducting young women, Clarice must navigate the unsettling mind of Lecter
to gain insights into the psyche of her target. The novel delves into themes of power,
manipulation, and the nature of evil, blending tension with profound character study.
